Welcome to the Yacht Rock Explorer!

This site models the relationships between Yacht Rockers and the songs that they performed. A large and invaluable thank you to the original judges of Yachtiness at Beyond Yacht Rock. None of this would be possible without their dedication and snarky judgement.

You can explore the reach of individual artists or map one artist to another. Do note that the amount of data returned and displayed may slow down your browser. It's recommended you try smaller return sets before working your way up to large conected networks.

Controls

The left panel includes controls for creating network diagrams under various criteria. At present, only exact text matches are accepted.

  • Toggle Labels - Hides/shows node labels.
  • Search - Search for an artist, and use the slider to determine how many hops out you would like to represent in the graph.
    • slider - Decides how many 'hops' from the initial Yachter to represent.
  • Draw Map - Graph the quickest path between two performers.
  • Zoom in and out of the map using mousewheel or trackpad.
